# Radio FEM - Play Store Materials (v0.2)

## 1) Listing text (English)

### App name (max 30)
Radio FEM

### Short description (max 80)
Live radio, podcasts, and weekly/monthly schedule in one app.

### Full description (suggested)
Listen to Radio FEM live and keep everything in one place.

With the Radio FEM app you can:
- Listen to the live stream with playback controls.
- See what is currently playing.
- Check the schedule in weekly and monthly views.
- Browse podcasts and play episodes inside the app.
- Control playback from notifications, headphones, and Bluetooth.
- Return to live radio with one tap after listening to podcasts.

Built for a simple and fast experience:
- Clean and responsive interface.
- Swipe navigation between tabs (Live, Schedule, Podcasts).
- Podcast progress bar and seek controls.

Take Radio FEM with you wherever you go.

## 2) Graphic assets checklist

### Required assets
- App icon:
  - 512 x 512 px
  - 32-bit PNG (alpha allowed)
  - Max 1024 KB
- Feature graphic:
  - 1024 x 500 px
  - JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha)
- Screenshots:
  - Minimum 2 screenshots to publish
  - JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha)
  - Min dimension: 320 px
  - Max dimension: 3840 px
  - Long side cannot be more than 2x the short side

### Highly recommended (better visibility)
- At least 4 phone screenshots:
  - Portrait: minimum 1080 x 1920 (9:16)
  - Landscape: minimum 1920 x 1080 (16:9)
- Keep screenshots as real UI (no device frames, no fake badges, no misleading text).
