## Description

Radio FEM is the official app of an independent and collaborative radio project created by Forro em Milao.

Our mission is to connect people through Forro culture with a curated, high-quality, and research-driven musical experience. The station is built with artistic freedom and no commercial pressure, so the focus stays on cultural value, careful selection, and community.

With the Radio FEM app, you can:

- Listen to the live stream anytime.
- See what is playing now.
- Browse the program schedule in weekly and monthly views.
- Explore podcasts and play episodes inside the app.
- Return from podcast playback to the live stream in one tap.
- Contact the Radio FEM team directly from the app.

Why Radio FEM:

- Independent and collaborative radio identity.
- Strong commitment to Brazilian roots and Forro culture.
- Non-commercial curation focused on quality and authenticity.
- A global bridge for listeners who love Forro.

Take Radio FEM with you and stay connected to the sound, stories, and spirit of Forro.

## Build / Archive

1. Open `ios/Runner.xcworkspace` in Xcode on a Mac.
2. Select the `Runner` target and confirm automatic signing with your Apple team.
3. Archive the app for `Any iOS Device`.
4. Export with [ExportOptions-AppStore.plist](/mnt/d/Radio_FEM_App/ios/ExportOptions-AppStore.plist) or upload directly through Xcode Organizer.
