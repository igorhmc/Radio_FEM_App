Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

1. Who we are

Radio FEM is an independent and collaborative radio project focused on forro culture, quality curation, artistic freedom, and strong community and cultural values.

This document explains how the Radio FEM Android app handles data.

2. Quick summary

  • The app does not require account creation and does not require login.
  • The app does not directly collect personal data (such as name, email, phone number, ID documents, or precise location).
  • The app does not use ad SDKs and does not display ads.
  • The app uses internet access to stream live audio and fetch schedule/podcast information.

3. Data processed by the app

The app only accesses public operational content:

  • radio now-playing status;
  • schedule;
  • podcasts and episodes;
  • live audio stream.

This data comes from the radio's public AzuraCast infrastructure and is not used to identify users inside the app.

4. App permissions

The app uses:

  • INTERNET: to load stream, schedule, and podcasts;
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE and FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK: to support continuous playback and media notification controls.

The app does not request sensitive permissions such as contacts, camera, recording microphone, location, or SMS.

5. Third-party services and external links

The app may open:

  • external links (for example, public podcast feed pages);
  • an email app for contacting the radio team.

When you leave the app to third-party services/sites, their own privacy policies apply.

6. Technical logs and infrastructure

Like any online service, infrastructure providers (hosting, CDN, streaming server, Play Store, Android OS) may process technical data such as IP address, timestamp, and device metadata for security, content delivery, and service reliability.

Radio FEM does not use this data for commercial profiling within the app.

7. Children

The app is not specifically directed to children under 13 and does not intentionally collect children's personal data.

8. Security

We adopt reasonable technical and organizational measures to support service security and reduce unauthorized access risks.

9. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect app improvements, technical changes, or legal/regulatory requirements. The current version is always the one published at the official URL provided in Google Play.
